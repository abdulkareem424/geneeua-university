Admissions

Geneva International University Online Program

Introduction

Geneva International University offers a comprehensive range of online programs, including Diplomas, Bachelor's, Master's, and Ph.D. degrees. The following terms outline the admission rules and specific requirements for each program level.

Admission Requirements

General Admission Requirements

All applicants must:

  • Demonstrate proficiency in English, the language of instruction.
  • Have access to a reliable internet connection and a computer that meets the university's technical specifications for online learning.
  • Submit a completed application form along with the non-refundable application fee.

Admissions Process

Specific Program Requirements

Undergraduate Programs

Diploma Programs:
  • Completion of secondary education or equivalent.
  • Submission of high school transcripts.
  • Personal statement outlining the applicant's educational goals and reasons for choosing the program.
Bachelor's Programs:
  • Completion of secondary education with a minimum GPA as specified by the program.
  • High school transcripts.
  • Personal statement.
  • Letters of recommendation (optional but recommended).

Postgraduate Programs

Master's Programs:
  • A bachelor's degree from an accredited institution.
  • Transcripts from all post-secondary institutions attended.
  • A minimum GPA as specified by the specific master's program.
  • A professional resume detailing any relevant work experience.
  • A statement of purpose that addresses the applicant's research interests and career goals.
  • Two letters of recommendation from academic or professional sources.
Ph.D. Programs:
  • A master's degree with a strong academic record from an accredited institution.
  • Official transcripts from all post-secondary education.
  • Evidence of research capability (e.g., a master's thesis, publications, or comprehensive research proposal).
  • A detailed research proposal aligning with the university's research strengths.
  • Three letters of recommendation, with at least two from academic advisors or professors familiar with the applicant's research.

Our university is committed to fostering growth, innovation, and excellence in education. Join us on this exciting journey of personal and academic development.
