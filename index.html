How it Works

Each student at GENEVA IU has a personal, customized path that is specifically built for their goals, skill level, and pace. Regardless of where they are located around the world, our students receive quality education from licensed teachers who are always available through online channels. We’re here to provide the flexible learning experience you need.

About Geneva International University

Here at Geneva International University, we are committed to pioneering the integration of tradition and innovation within the realm of online education. Founded in 2016 initially as the Quality of Life Institute in Lebanon and reestablished in December 2023 as Geneva International University, we have broadened our scope to provide extensive online undergraduate and postgraduate programs worldwide. Our commitment to enhancing educational accessibility has evolved into a dedication to upholding global education standards and achieving online learning excellence. Geneva International University is dedicated to offering educational programs that meet and surpass the highest standards of academic excellence. Our vibrant online learning environment is enriched by expert faculty members who are focused on equipping students from varied backgrounds with the knowledge, skills, and competencies needed to excel in their chosen fields and to make significant contributions to their communities.​ Thank you for choosing Geneva International University. We look forward to supporting your educational journey and celebrating your future successes. Warm regards, Dr. S. Al Hariri Founder, International University Of Geneva

OUR GOALS:

  • Excellence in Education: To cultivate and reward excellence in the acquisition, advancement, and dissemination of knowledge.
  • Personal and Professional Fulfillment: To guide students in applying their knowledge and skills towards achieving personal and professional fulfillment.
  • Cultural Appreciation: To foster an appreciation of the historical and ongoing development of human culture.
  • Critical Skills Development: To develop critical thinking, effective communication, and lifelong learning skills.
  • Ethical Values: To promote the values of ethical behavior, responsibility, and commitment
  • Diverse Learning Opportunities: To provide academic programs, services, facilities, and technologies that offer diverse opportunities for learning.
  • Qualified Educators: To recruit and retain a diverse faculty of qualified educators, providing an environment conducive to their growth as teachers and scholars.
  • Intercultural Understanding: To foster intercultural understanding, preparing students to be active members of the global community.
  • Economic and Societal Contribution: To contribute to the economic and societal development of Geneva, Switzerland, and beyond.
